One-Time Pad Assignment

Practice Questions

Basic Concepts (20 points)

  1. Explain why the One-Time Pad is considered perfectly secure when implemented correctly.
  2. List and explain the three essential requirements for a One-Time Pad key.
  3. What are the main practical limitations of using One-Time Pad in real-world applications?
  4. Describe the importance of true randomness in OTP key generation.

Encryption Exercise (40 points)

Using the One-Time Pad method, encrypt the following message:

Plaintext: "CRYPTOGRAPHY"

Key: "QWERTASDFGH"

  • Show your work step by step
  • Include the numerical conversions
  • Show the modular arithmetic
  • Present the final ciphertext

Decryption Challenge (40 points)

Decrypt the following message:

Ciphertext: "XMELVPSKT"

Key: "SECRETKEY"

  • Show complete decryption process
  • Explain each step
  • Verify your answer
